# Workforce-Analytics
This project analyzes workforce data to show staff distribution by LGAs and senatorial Districts, support promotion planning, retirement, and leave tracking. Using spreadsheet-based analytics, the project helps HR teams identify staff due or overdue for promotion, detect career stagnation, and plan for workforce exits.

BUSINESS PROBLEM
Human resource units often rely on manual processes to:

Track promotion eligibility
Identify overdue promotions
Monitor stagnation in grade progression
Plan for retirements
Ensure proper leave utilization

These challenges can lead to delays, poor workforce planning, and compliance risks.

DATA DESCRIPTION

The dataset contains employee records with the following attributes:
Employee ID
Name
Grade Level
Date of Last Promotion
Local Government Area (LGA)
Date of Retirement
Leave Balance / Leave Consummation Status

TOOLS & TECHNIQUES

Microsoft Excel
Data Cleaning & Validation
Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
Lookup Functions (VLOOKUP / XLOOKUP)
Date & Logical Functions
Conditional Formatting

KEY ANALYSES

Aggregation of staff by LGA & their demographics
Aggregation of staff by Senatorial Districts & their demographics
Employees due for promotion by the year
Identification of overdue promotions
Detection of stagnated employees
Workforce distribution by LGA & Senatorial District
Retirement tracking and forecasting
Leave consummation monitoring

INSIGHTS GENERATED

Highlighted staff overdue for promotion across multiple LGAs
Identified grade-level stagnation patterns
Provided retirement outlooks for workforce planning
Flagged unused leave balances requiring HR action
